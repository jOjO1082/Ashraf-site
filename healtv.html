About Services HealTV Our Clients

The HealTV Group comprises a team of young, brilliant, and resourceful Nigerian students committed to upholding the moral values of society through impactful media. We believe that creativity does not require the compromise of sanity or values; rather, it can entertain while simultaneously enriching the minds of audiences.

In a challenging economic environment, we recognize that resourceful Nigerians must remain proactive to thrive. For this reason, we actively engage young people, fostering a progressive team united by the vision of reversing societal decadence and promoting positive change. Through this approach, we empower young people with practical skills while offering hope and direction.

Despite limited resources, we have consistently produced high-quality Ramadan programming each year, with a growing body of work that includes over 50 videos on YouTube and more than 100 pieces of content on Instagram. However, to sustain and expand our efforts beyond Ramadan, we are in constant need of support. Such support will enable us to fairly remunerate our contributors, acquire additional equipment, train more young creatives, and secure airtime on television channels.
